GUIDE:


there are several ways to do consciousness dumping, but the three most common ones are as follows:

train-tracking

if you have an internal monologue, then find a stray train of thought, follow it, and type it in real time. usually people do this for a set amount of time, but you don't have to. if you don't have one, then just let yourself relax, find something to focus on, and let a thought write itself out.

object-logging

this one is simple - pick a word or an object, then follow it with a word that you associate with said word/object, then do the same thing with the next word, and so on. a good example of this is something like : "train tracks shoes feet pain bones marrow sand oasis death freedom climbing falling (...)"

home-base

It's called home-base because you're creating a home in your head and basing your writing around it. think up a house, or a building, or a sterile laboratory, or an esoteric facility, and document yourself exploring it. this is fun to do with a clock, but again, you don't have to.
