eukaryote

yoo͞-kăr′ē-ōt, -ē-ət

the eukaryotes constitute the domain of eukaryota - organisms whose cells have a nucleus. all animals, plants, fungi, and many unicellular organisms are eukaryotes. they constitute a major group of life forms alongside the two groups of prokaryotes: the bacteria and the archaea.
